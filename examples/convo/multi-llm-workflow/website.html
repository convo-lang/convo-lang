How It Works: 3 Easy Steps

1

Hook & Measure

Attach your frame to FrameMate Pro using the adjustable hook guides. Slide to match the distance between your frame's hang points.

2

Level

Hold the tool against the wall and align using the dual bubble vials (horizontal and vertical) or the center-finding scale for perfect centering.

3

Mark & Hang

Press to leave precise marks with the wall-safe pins. Hammer in nails/anchors on the marks, then hang your frame — perfectly level.
