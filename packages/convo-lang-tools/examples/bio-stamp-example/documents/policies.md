# Bio Stamp Company Policies

## 1. Code of Conduct
- Employees must act with honesty, integrity, and respect in all interactions.
- Discrimination, harassment, or retaliation of any kind is strictly prohibited.
- Confidential company and client information must not be shared outside authorized channels.
- Conflicts of interest must be promptly reported to management.

## 2. Data Privacy and Security
- Every employee is responsible for protecting the privacy and security of DNA and personal data.
- All usage of sensitive data must comply with relevant laws (e.g., GDPR, HIPAA).
- Secure passwords and multi-factor authentication are mandatory for all digital systems.
- Any suspected breach or data incident must be reported immediately to the Data Security department.
- Physical access to labs and data centers is restricted to authorized personnel only.

## 3. Research, Development, and Product Integrity
- Research and development must adhere to scientific rigor, transparency, and best ethical practices.
- All products undergo thorough quality assurance and must meet legal and regulatory requirements prior to release.
- All experiments, clinical studies, and data analyses require full documentation and approval from the relevant oversight bodies.

## 4. Regulatory Compliance
- All employees must comply with local, national, and international regulations impacting biotechnology, genetic data, and digital privacy.
- The Compliance Team conducts regular audits; cooperation is mandatory.
- Training on relevant laws and company procedures is required and will be provided on a recurring basis.

## 5. Intellectual Property
- All inventions, discoveries, processes, and data created during employment are property of Bio Stamp.
- Employees must promptly disclose inventions and cooperate in securing intellectual property rights.
- Unauthorized sharing or use of proprietary information is grounds for disciplinary action.

## 6. Use of Company Resources
- Company devices, software, and facilities are to be used only for authorized business purposes.
- Personal use must not interfere with company operations or security.
- No unauthorized installation of hardware or software is allowed on company devices.

## 7. Workplace Health, Safety, and Environment
- Employees must follow all laboratory safety protocols and wear appropriate personal protective equipment.
- Report unsafe conditions, accidents, or hazards immediately to Facilities & Operations.
- Sustainability and environmental responsibility are essential in all operations.

## 8. Equal Opportunity and Inclusion
- We are committed to a diverse, equitable, and inclusive workplace.
- Hiring, promotions, and compensation are based solely on qualifications, performance, and merit.
- Reports of bias or discrimination will be investigated promptly and confidentially.

## 9. Remote Work and Flexible Schedules
- Roles that permit remote work or flexible hours must agree to expectations for availability, communication, and data security.
- Employees must ensure a secure, distraction-free, and confidential work environment when working remotely.

## 10. Confidentiality and Communications
- All internal and external communications should reflect Bio Stamp’s values and maintain confidentiality.
- Public statements, media contact, or disclosure of company information require prior approval from the Executive or Legal Affairs teams.

## 11. Reporting Violations
- Employees may report any concerns or policy violations anonymously via the company’s whistleblower hotline.
- Retaliation against those reporting concerns in good faith is prohibited.

## 12. Disciplinary Actions
- Violations of company policy may result in verbal or written warnings, suspension, or termination, depending on severity.
- Gross misconduct (e.g., data theft, discrimination, violence) will result in immediate disciplinary action up to and including dismissal.

---

*These policies are reviewed annually and may be updated as laws, regulations, and business needs evolve. By working at Bio Stamp, you agree to uphold these principles to support our mission of responsible biological authentication.*
