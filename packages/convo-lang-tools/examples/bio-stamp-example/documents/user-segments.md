# User Segments

1) Industry vertical segments
- Banking/Financial Services: Retail/wealth banks, payments, fintech, brokerages, insurance (fraud reduction, KYC/AML, high-value approvals).
- Healthcare: Hospitals/IDNs, telehealth, labs, payers, clinical trials (patient matching, consent, EHR integrity).
- Government/Defense/Public Safety: Federal, state, defense, law enforcement (classified access, workforce vetting, e-gov services).
- Travel/Border/Aviation: Airports, border agencies, airlines (passenger identity, fast lanes, staff access).
- Enterprise/Cloud/Tech: SaaS, cloud providers, data centers, critical infrastructure (privileged access, zero trust IAM).
- Pharma/Biotech/Research: R&D labs, CROs, biobanks (chain-of-custody, participant verification).
- Legal/Notary/Regulated Services: e-notary, legal tech, title/escrow (non-repudiation, fraud prevention).

2) Buyer and stakeholder personas
- Economic buyers: CIO/CTO, COO, line-of-business leaders.
- Security/compliance buyers: CISO, DPO/CPO, General Counsel, Risk/Compliance heads.
- Technical evaluators: IAM architects, security engineers, IT infrastructure, data engineering.
- Operations owners: Fraud ops, clinical ops, airport ops, facilities/access control.
- Developers/integrators: Backend/API engineers, solution architects.
- Procurement/finance: Vendor management, sourcing.

3) End-user segments (who authenticates)
- Consumers/customers: Banking clients, patients, citizens accessing e-gov services.
- Workforce: Employees, contractors, privileged admins, clinicians, airport/port staff.
- Participants/travelers: Clinical trial participants, passengers.
- Field agents/first responders: Government and critical response personnel.

4) Use-case segments
- Remote onboarding/KYC and account opening.
- High-value transaction approvals and step-up auth.
- Account recovery/credential reset.
- Workforce and privileged access (logical and physical).
- Patient identity, consent, and record linkage.
- Chain-of-custody and sample provenance.
- Border control and secure travel corridors.
- Audit/compliance attestation (who did what, provably).

5) Deployment/IT segments
- Cloud-native SaaS adopters needing fast integration (APIs/SDKs).
- Hybrid enterprises with data residency needs.
- On-prem/air-gapped sovereign deployments (defense/government).
- Managed service customers seeking end-to-end operations.

6) Adoption maturity segments
- Innovation-led early adopters (pilots, labs, POCs).
- Risk-driven adopters post-incident (breach/fraud response).
- Compliance-driven adopters (ahead of audits/regulatory milestones).
- Efficiency/TCO-driven adopters (consolidating MFA/ID stacks).

7) Geography/regulatory segments
- EU/UK (GDPR/UK GDPR, Schrems II, data residency).
- US (HIPAA, GLBA, state privacy laws).
- APAC (PDPA, Australian Privacy Act, Japan APPI).
- Middle East and sovereign cloud requirements.

8) Organization size/complexity
- Enterprise/Global 2000 (complex IAM estates, custom integrations).
- Upper mid-market (standardized platforms, faster sales cycles).
- Public sector agencies (formal procurement, pilots to scale).

9) Partner ecosystem segments
- Identity platforms and SSO/IAM providers (Okta, Azure AD/Entra, Ping).
- Cloud/Hyperscalers (AWS, Azure, GCP) and marketplace buyers.
- Systems integrators/consultancies (security, healthcare IT).
- Device/OEM partners (kiosks, access control hardware).
- Healthcare IT vendors (EHR/LIS, HIEs).
