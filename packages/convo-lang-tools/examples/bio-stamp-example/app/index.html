How it works

A privacy‑preserving pipeline from enrollment to authentication—designed for speed, accuracy, and non‑repudiation.

1) Enroll

Collect a simple saliva/cheek swab using our kit. Consent is captured with auditable records. Chain‑of‑custody options available.

2) Create DNA Signature

We extract a minimal, non‑reversible DNA signature from select markers—not a full genome—then encrypt and bind it to your identity profile.

3) Authenticate

For logins, step‑up checks, or physical access, a quick sample is matched against the signature in seconds and policy decisions are enforced.

4) Govern & Audit

Role‑based access, data residency controls, and complete audit trails support regulatory reporting and investigations.

Bio Stamp does not store full genomes and cannot reconstruct an individual’s genome from our signatures.

Frequently asked questions

How fast is authentication?

Typical matches complete in seconds, depending on policy and network conditions. Offline/air‑gapped modes are also supported.

Do you store full genomes?

No. We store a minimal, non‑reversible DNA signature derived from select markers. It cannot reconstruct an individual’s genome.

Who owns the data?

We operate with user consent and clear data control options. Customers retain control over their environment and can enforce residency, retention, and deletion policies.

What about compliance?

Bio Stamp is designed to support requirements under GDPR/UK GDPR, HIPAA, and similar laws. We provide audit logs, DSAR workflows, and BAA support where applicable.

Can we integrate with existing IAM?

Yes. Pre‑built connectors and SDKs integrate with major identity platforms, access control devices, and clinical systems.

What if connectivity is limited?

Use hybrid or fully offline deployments with local policy enforcement and periodic sync when available.
