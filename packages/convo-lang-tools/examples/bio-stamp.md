## Company Description:
Bio Stamp combines the frontier of biological science with cutting-edge computing to make identity both unique and secure—empowering humans with true biological authentication. Each department plays a crucial role in delivering this vision reliably and responsibly.


## Departments

### Genomics Research & Development
Oversees ongoing innovation in DNA analysis technology to ensure secure, accurate, and rapid identification. Develops protocols for DNA data collection, storage, and authentication methods.

### Data Security and Privacy
Responsible for protecting sensitive DNA and identity data against breaches. Implements encryption, access controls, compliance with privacy laws (e.g., GDPR, HIPAA), and responds to security incidents.

### Data Engineering & IT Infrastructure
Designs and maintains the robust infrastructure required to store and process large-scale DNA data securely. Ensures uptime, scalability, and integrity of authentication systems.

### Bioinformatics
Processes raw DNA data, develops matching algorithms, and ensures reliable biostatistical methods to identify individuals uniquely. Provides technical support for DNA-based authentication.

### Product Management
Works across departments to shape the company’s DNA authentication solutions, defines customer requirements, and oversees product development cycles from conception to launch.

### Software Development
Creates and maintains user interfaces, APIs, and backend systems enabling DNA-based authentication. Collaborates with R&D, bioinformatics, and data security teams to deliver secure and user-friendly applications.

### Quality Assurance & Compliance
Ensures all products and processes meet legal, regulatory, and quality standards. Performs audits, manages certifications, and coordinates with regulatory agencies regarding genetic data usage.

### Customer Support & Services
Provides technical assistance and education to clients, helping them integrate Bio Stamp’s authentication services and addressing product usage or troubleshooting issues promptly.

### Business Development & Partnerships
Identifies market opportunities, forms strategic partnerships, and expands the business into new verticals such as banking, healthcare, government, and travel security.

### Sales & Marketing
Promotes Bio Stamp’s unique value proposition, acquires new clients, and manages relationships with existing customers. Develops marketing materials and coordinates public outreach.

### Legal Affairs
Advises on contracts, intellectual property, privacy policies, and regulatory compliance. Manages risk and liaises with authorities to ensure lawful use of DNA identity data.

### Human Resources (HR)
Handles recruitment, onboarding, training, payroll, and employee wellbeing. Fosters a compliant, diverse, and innovative workplace culture.

### Finance & Accounting
Manages budgets, financial reporting, payroll, and ensures fiscal health to support the company’s growth and operations.

### Facilities & Operations
Maintains office, laboratory, and data center environments. Ensures smooth day-to-day operation, manages supplies, and oversees compliance with safety and environmental protocols.

### Executive Leadership & Strategy
Sets overall vision, mission, and strategy. Makes high-level business decisions, steers company direction, and ensures alignment of departmental goals with company objectives.
